How it works

From scattered files to instant answers

1

Connect your files

Link your Google Drive folder, Gmail inbox, or Calendar. Oncofiles watches for new medical files automatically.

2

AI reads everything

Every document gets OCR, summaries, tags, diagnoses, and structured metadata. Scans become searchable text in minutes.

3

Ask naturally

In Claude or ChatGPT, just ask: "What did my last blood test show?" Your AI has full context from all your records.

What it does

Your medical files, finally understood

📄

Reads every format

PDFs, scanned images, photos of documents. AI extracts text from anything and organizes it into 15 medical categories.

🔬

Tracks your labs

Lab values are parsed, stored, and tracked over time. Get trends, safety checks before chemo cycles, and alerts on abnormal results.

📅

Knows your timeline

Treatment events, appointments, and milestones are connected to your documents. Ask about any date and get full context.

🔒

Your data, your control

Self-hosted. Nothing leaves your infrastructure. No third-party cloud, no data sharing. Privacy by architecture.

✉

Scans Gmail too

Medical emails from doctors, labs, and hospitals are automatically detected, classified, and linked to your documents.

🔄

Always in sync

Bidirectional Google Drive sync every 5 minutes. New files are processed, renamed, and organized without lifting a finger.
