Ako to funguje

Od roztrúsených súborov k okamžitým odpovediam

1

Prepojte svoje súbory

Prepojte Google Drive priečinok, Gmail alebo Kalendár. Oncofiles automaticky sleduje nové zdravotné dokumenty.

2

AI prečíta všetko

Každý dokument dostane OCR, zhrnutie, diagnózy a štruktúrované metadáta. Naskenované dokumenty sa stanú prehľadávateľným textom za minúty.

3

Pýtajte sa prirodzene

V Claude alebo ChatGPT sa jednoducho opýtajte: „Čo ukázali moje posledné odbery?“ Vaše AI má plný kontext zo všetkých záznamov.

What it does

Your medical files, finally understood

📄

Reads every format

PDFs, scanned images, photos of documents. AI extracts text from anything and organizes it into 15 medical categories.

🔬

Tracks your labs

Lab values are parsed, stored, and tracked over time. Get trends, safety checks before chemo cycles, and alerts on abnormal results.

📅

Reads your Calendar

Google Calendar appointments are scanned and classified. Chemo cycles, lab visits, and consults become treatment events with full context.

🔒

Your data, your control

Self-hosted. Nothing leaves your infrastructure. No third-party cloud, no data sharing. Privacy by architecture.

✉

Scans Gmail too

Medical emails from doctors, labs, and hospitals are automatically detected, classified, and linked to your documents.

🔄

Always in sync

Bidirectional Google Drive sync every 5 minutes. New files are processed, renamed, and organized without lifting a finger.
